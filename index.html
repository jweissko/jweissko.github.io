Meet Jonathan Weisskoff

  • Head Teaching Assistant for CS1270: Database Management Systems
  • Brown University Resuming Undergraduate
  • Sc.B. Computer Science Candidate
  • Former Rabbi and Life Coach

Background

Hailing from Boston, Massachusetts, Jon spent years after high school pursuing traditional Jewish learning, and set out to begin his career in rabbinics, but felt that his views had grown to be too universalistic to follow a particular belief doctrine. So he made a 180, and attempted an entrepreneurial adventure as a life coach, and then came to Brown University for a second bachelors degree. He settled on computer science at the start of his sophomore year.

Current Work

Jon has had the good fortune of serving as Head Teaching Assistant for CS1270: Database Management Systems at Brown University, under Professor Stan Zdonik this past Fall semester (2019). While the course content needed an enourmous amount of work, he is proud to report that the students are taking away from the course a deeper understanding of databases this year than his own cohort did the year before. He looks forward to continuing to serve as a Teaching Assistant at Brown University next semester, for the course CS1951A: Data Science.

Future

Jon hopes to work as a software engineer after his graduation. He enjoys working with all things databases.
