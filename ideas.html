Interests & Hobbies

Coding

Jon likes coding software. On a good day, all the parts to come together smoothly.

Connecting

Jon likes connecting with people. He is involved with Reevaluation Counseling, which is a form of peer counseling.

Exercising

Jon works out at the gym, and sometimes listens to audiobooks between sets. He's painstakingly making his way through Thomas Khun's "The Structure of Scientific Revolution."

Baking Bread

Jon likes baking whole-grain sourdough breads on weekends. He uses home-ground Kamut, rye, spelt, oats and rice flour. He likes its earthy taste!


Personal Values

Awareness

"Self-awareness allows one to discover the capacity to persevere through challenges. Lack thereof causes suffering."

Authenticity

"Authenticy is born of awareness, for when one realizes that most habituated defenses are unwarranted, one can relax them and be self-revealing. Constricted awareness causes rigid, repetitive behaviors that do not express the expansiveness of the heart."

Love

"Caring and affection are perhaps the most fulfilling emotions. Through caring one learns to expand into something more than oneself."

Healing

"Processing stress allows one to make decisions unpolarized by reactivity."

Seeking Truth

"Seeking truth requires independent thinking. To become an independent thinker, one needs to learn to trust in one's ability to make wise decisions."

Family

"One of the strongest ways to express love is though family bonds. Caring that one receives and gives in a family context drives one's growth as a person."


Metaphysical Beliefs

Interconnectedness

"Nothing exists in absolute isolation from the whole (Spinoza)."

Events

"Alfred North Whitehead's "Process Philosophy" posits that there are only events; there is no concrete material. What appears to be material is in actuality highly patterned events that have become so cyclical that they appear concrete."

Mentation

"Alfred North Whiteheads's theory of mind and body posits that both mentation and the physical are events of an organic universe (contra contemporary physicalists) whose only constant forces are creativity and entropy (Rupert Sheldrake, "Science Set Free", based on A.N. Whitehead, above)."

Decisions

"Every decision sets a force into motion towards its actuation, and alters the decider's psychic constitution. Through decisions, one participates in one's evolutionary trajectory."
